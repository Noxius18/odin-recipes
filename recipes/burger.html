Simple Hamburger Stroganoff

Creamy and easy to make

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounche) package egg noodles
  • 1 pound lean beef ground
  • 1 (8 ounche) package cream cheese, cut into pieces
  • 1 (6 ounche) can chopped mushrooms, with liquid
  • 1(.75 ounche) packet dry brown gravy mix
  • 2 (10.5 ounche) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 (8 ounche) container sour cream
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, 7 to 9 minutes. Drain.
  2. At the same time, cook ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes;
    drain and discard grease. Stir in cream cheese, mushrooms with liquid, and gravy mix;
    cook and stir over medium heat until cream cheese melts, 2 to 3 minutes. Add condensed soup, sour cream, and milk;
    cook, stirring occasionally, until smooth and creamy, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Drain egg noodles; stir into the beef mixture. Cook until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
